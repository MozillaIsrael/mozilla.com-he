Why Get Weave Sync?

Typing is hard

Getting Started

  1. Install Weave Sync on your PC

    Make sure your PC runs Firefox 3.5 or higher and your phone is a Firefox-tested device (upgrade Firefox).

    • Download Weave Sync
    • After the download is complete, click “Restart Firefox” to finish installing the Weave Sync Add-on.
    • When Firefox restarts, use the “Tools” menu or the Weave Sync icon on the bottom-right of the status-bar to open Weave Sync and sign in.

  2. Install the Weave Sync add-on on your mobile

    • Go your Preferences, select the add-ons button.
    • Search for and select the Weave Sync add-on. Sign in, sync, and away you go.
